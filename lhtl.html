LEARNING HOW TO LEARN

GO BACK TO HOME

This course provides easy access to the valuable learning techniques used by specialists in art, music, literature, math, science, sports, and a variety of other fields. We'll discover how the brain encapsulates ("chunks") information and uses two very different learning processes. We'll also go through learning illusions, memory strategies, how to deal with procrastination, and research-backed best practices for mastering difficult subjects. You can transform your thinking and your life by using these methods, regardless of your skill levels in the topics you want to master. If you're a seasoned learner, this look inside the hood will provide you with advice for accelerating your performance, including counterintuitive test-taking strategies and insights that will help you make the most of your time on homework and problem sets.
