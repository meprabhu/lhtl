The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) is a private university in Dehradun, India. UPES has been ranked amongst the top 100 universities in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2021. In the same list, UPES School of Law is ranked amongst the top 25 Law Colleges, the UPES School of Business is featured amongst the top 50 Management Institutes, and UPES School of Engineering lists amongst top 100 Engineering Universities, across India.
